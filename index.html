RESEARCH INTERESTS

My research areas mainly include Cybersecurity, Data Mining, Machine Learning, and Health Intelligence. I have contributed to over 30 publications in my field, collectively amassing more than 1500 citations on Google Scholar. This includes work published in KDD (3 papers), AAAI (2 papers), IJCAI (3 papers), WWW (2 papers), CIKM (2 papers), and ACSAC (2 papers). By working with my team, I have received the SIGKDD 2017 Best Paper Award and SIGKDD 2017 Best Student Paper Award (Applied Data Science Track) and the MetroLab Innovation of the Month (2020).
